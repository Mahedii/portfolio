Summary

Dark, mysterious and lunar photo series created for the latest collection of tables and new terrazzo colors from Béton Architectural Johnstone, a specialist in high-end concrete architecture in Quebec. With its sharp play of shadows and light, photography highlights the details of the concrete materials and the new organic colors of the furniture, where the strength and beauty of the materials contrast with the arid and enigmatic environment of the landscape.

THE APPROACH

This dark, mysterious, quasi-lunar photo series was created to showcase the latest collection of tables and new terrazzo colours from Béton Architectural Johnstone, a specialist in high-end architectural concrete in Québec. The razor-sharp interplay of light and shadow featured in these photographs highlights the details of the concrete materials and the new organic furniture shades, contrasting the strength and beauty of the materials with the stark, enigmatic landscape.
